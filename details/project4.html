Power Market Modelling and Optimization

The goal was simulate a power market through simulations and optimize the alocation of resoucres by solving a stochastic model with Conditional Value at Risk (CVaR) constraints. The script models energy production and trading across two regions using two technologies under three different demand and capacity scenarios. By adding more regions and technologies this script can become more complex and realistic but the goal was to showcase the approach

Tools used: Python, Gurobi
